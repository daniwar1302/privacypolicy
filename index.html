Chamba Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

For Users in the United States

Laws in California and other states provide users with certain rights regarding their personal information, including the right to access, delete, correct and/or opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of their personal information. This page provides information on how users in the United States may exercise their rights under these laws.

All users can make choices about how we use your data, explore privacy features, and manage privacy settings in our Privacy Center.

Our Policy On Selling and Sharing Data

Laws in California and other states give users in the state the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of their personal information. "Sale" refers to the disclosure of personal information to a third party in exchange for money or other value. "Share" refers to the disclosure of personal information to a third party for purposes of targeted advertising.

Chamba discloses user personal information to third parties to enable personalized advertising on apps, websites or other properties owned by Chamba or other companies. Such disclosures may be considered "sales" or "sharing" of personal information under the laws described above. Users in the United States may opt-out of these below.

If you opt out, Chamba will not disclose your personal information to third parties for purposes of targeted advertising.

U.S. User Data Rights

What personal data does Chamba collect and how do we use it?

  • Account Information:
    • Email address
    • Full name
    • Profile picture (optional)
    • Phone number (optional)
  • Location Data:
    • Current location (to find nearby services)
    • Saved locations (for quick booking)
  • Usage Data:
    • Booking history
    • Service preferences
    • App interaction patterns
    • Search history

What rights do users in the United States have?

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

How do I exercise my rights?

You can exercise your rights by:

  • Using our Privacy Center
  • Contacting our support team
  • Submitting a request through our app

How will Chamba verify my identity?

We verify your identity by:

  • Matching information you provide with our records
  • Requiring additional verification for sensitive requests
  • Using secure authentication methods

Can an authorized agent submit a request on my behalf?

Yes, you can authorize someone to make a request on your behalf. The authorized agent must provide:

  • Written permission from you
  • Verification of their identity
  • Verification of your identity

Can I submit an appeal if Chamba declines my request?

Yes, you can appeal our decision within 30 days of receiving our response. We will respond to your appeal within 60 days.

Does Chamba use de-identified data?

We may de-identify your personal information and use it for:

  • Product research and development
  • Service improvement
  • Analytics and reporting

We will not attempt to re-identify any individual while using de-identified information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • We use secure servers and cloud storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data
